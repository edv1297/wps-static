Hello, my name is William, and photography is my passion. I have been taking photographs since I was 20 years old in Cuba. Since then, a lot has changed. Photography went from film to digital. There have been several trends that I have witnesses and learned from as time has passed. After emigratting to the U.S. I worked several jobs while continuing to do some photography work on the side. Eventually, I built up my business and was able to do photography full time.

I love what I do because every new client is a creative journey. Starting with creating their custom package up until when the final product is delivered, I always try to better myself and innovate. Most clients claim that they xlove my work.

Thanks for visiting my site, if you have any questions please reach out. Hope to hear from you soon!
